Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a popular South Asian dish made with aromatic basmati rice, spices, and marinated meat, layered and cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 2 pounds chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon saffron threads, soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, halved (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, mix yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Add chicken and coat well. Set aside for 1 hour.
  2. Step 2: Heat oil in a large pan and fry onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish and set aside.
  3. Step 3: Add garlic and ginger paste to the pan and sauté for 1 minute. Add marinated chicken and cook until no longer pink, about 10 minutes.
  4. Step 4: In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, cumin seeds, and salt. Stir in soaked rice and cook until 70% done, then drain.
  5. Step 5: Layer the biryani: Spread half of the cooked rice in a large pot. Add the chicken mixture, fresh cilantro, mint, and the remaining rice. Drizzle with saffron milk and top with fried onions.
  6. Step 6: Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat (dum method) for 25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the rice to fully cook.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot, garnished with fried onions and hard-boiled eggs, if desired.
